Focaccia with caramelized Onions, Cranberries, and brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

INGREDIENTS

For the focaccia
  • 4 cups (500) all-purpose floor
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups(350G)
  • 1/4 Cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan
For the topping
  • 3 medium yellow onions thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 10 ounces brie , cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, thought it's not neccesary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

1. make the focaccia dough

In a medium-sized mixing bowl or in a stand mixer with paddle attachment, mix togheter all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absoorbed, You don,t need to actually knead the dough, though i sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour..
